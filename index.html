What is Ironveil

A world of iron,
built by hands like yours

Ironveil is an open-world VR crafting RPG where you survive, forge, and shape a living world — one ingot at a time.

Mine deep into unstable ruins. Smelt raw ore at your forge. Craft weapons with real hands-on VR mechanics that reward skill, not menus. The world reacts to what you build and who you become.

What Sets It Apart

Four reasons Ironveil is different

I
Hands-On VR Crafting

Every tool is made with your hands — no crafting menus, no floating interfaces. Heat metal, shape it on the anvil, quench and grind. The forge rewards technique.

II
A World That Changes

Ironveil's world evolves based on what players build and destroy. Mining collapses tunnels. Settlements rise where players forge alliances. Nothing is static.

III
Built in the Open

Every system, every sprint, every design decision is shared publicly. You're not just a player waiting — you're a witness to how a game gets made from scratch.

IV
One Developer, One Vision

No committee. No publisher pressures. Ironveil is built by a single developer with a clear creative direction — and a community that shapes what comes next.
